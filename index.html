What is Pixonoids?
Pixonoids is a platform for creativity, for fervent people who have a passion for photography,cinematography, graphic designing, and/or animation,web desiging. You all are welcome here to share your work, participate in our online competitions and have discussions on varied software. There is interviews before cultural fest known as Hill'fair and tech fest known as Nimbus for intersted students to join pixonoids. Pixonoids is passion,creativity,dhadhi fun.There are lot of things you can learn in our club like photography,animations,graphics design,web desiging
